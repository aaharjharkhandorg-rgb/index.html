Welcome to Aahar Jharkhand

Aahar Jharkhand is the official online platform of the Department of Food, Public Distribution & Consumer Affairs, Jharkhand. It provides transparent services like ration card search, NFSA allocation reports, and beneficiary verification.

Key Services

  • Ration Card Search & Verification
  • Monthly NFSA Allocation Reports
  • Beneficiary Transparency Portal
  • Dealer & Fair Price Shop Records

Why Choose Aahar Jharkhand?

The Aahar Jharkhand portal ensures food security under the National Food Security Act (NFSA) 2013, helping families access subsidized food grains in a fair and transparent manner.
